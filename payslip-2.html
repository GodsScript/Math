Grading Rubric

Criteria Excellent Good Fair Needs Improvement
1. Accuracy in Worksheet 1 All entries in Worksheet 1 are 100% correct.
(20 points)		 1–2 minor errors in computations or placement.
(16–19 points)		 3 or more errors in values or steps.
(10–15 points)		 Major errors or incomplete/missing entries.
(4–9 points)
2. Computation of Tax Due Tax due is computed correctly using accurate taxable income and tax rate.
(10 points)		 1–2 small errors in formula or tax table usage.
(8–9 points)		 3 or more errors affecting result.
(5–7 points)		 Incorrect or not computed at all.
(2–4 points)
3. Correctness of Worksheet 2 All Worksheet 2 values (gross, deductions, net income) are correct and complete.
(10 points)		 1–2 errors in computation or placement.
(8–9 points)		 3+ errors or inconsistencies in entries.
(5–7 points)		 Major errors or Worksheet 2 is incomplete.
(2–4 points)
4. Neatness and Submission Very neat, organized, and submitted on time.
(10 points)		 Mostly neat and submitted up to 1–2 minutes late.
(8 points)		 Messy or submitted 3–5 minutes late.
(6 points) 		Very messy/disorganized or submitted more than 5 minutes late.
(0–4 points)
